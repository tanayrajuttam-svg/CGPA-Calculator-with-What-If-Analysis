Everything you need

Built for every student

From first year to final semester, GradeIQ gives you complete visibility into your academic performance.

⬡
Live CGPA Tracking
Add courses with credits and grades. Your CGPA recalculates instantly using weighted grade point average — no manual math needed.
◇
What-If Analysis
Simulate any grade scenario before it happens. See exactly how a B+ instead of an A affects your overall CGPA — in real time.
◎
Target CGPA Calculator
Set your target CGPA and enter your upcoming semester credits. GradeIQ tells you exactly what SGPA you need to hit it.
▦
Semester Breakdown
See your SGPA for each semester individually. Understand which semesters pulled your CGPA up or down at a glance.
◈
SGPA Trend Chart
Visualize your semester-wise SGPA trend with an animated Chart.js line graph. Spot patterns and track your improvement over time.
⬇
PDF Transcript Export
Download a clean PDF of your full academic transcript — all semesters, all courses, your CGPA — in one shareable document.
